About me

My passion as a front-end web developer is to connect design with functionality.

Between 2015 to 2019, I worked at NAB (National Australia Bank)'s Digital Wealth department in various scrum teams alongside UX/UI designers and back-end developers to create the best experience for the user.

I am currently participating in the JET Programme as an ALT (Assistant Language Teacher) based in Tokyo, Japan. On the side, I work on personal coding projects which can be found on my GitHub.
